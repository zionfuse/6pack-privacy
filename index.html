Privacy Policy for Six Pack Builder

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

Six Pack Builder is an offline fitness app that helps users track and perform ab exercises to achieve six-pack abs. The app does not collect, share, or store any personal information online.

Information Collection and Use

This app does not require a login or an internet connection. All data related to your exercise progress is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is sent to any server or third-party service.

Data Security

Your workout progress and settings are stored only on your device. We do not have access to this data, and uninstalling the app will permanently delete it.
